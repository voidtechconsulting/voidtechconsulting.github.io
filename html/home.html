About Us

Founded in 2019, VOID Tech Consulting is the first student organization at the University of Michigan focused on technical client projects. Each year, new members learn web devlopment skills and get the opportunity to work with a project team, creating or improving a site for a real client. We partner with local businesses and non-profits to create valuable experiences for our members and amazing websites for our clients.

Get Involved

Product

Product Managers spearhead our client interactions and content strategy. In this cross-functional role, students develop their communication skills working with clients, designers and developers to ensure the product we produce meets all of our client's needs.

Development

In the fall semester, developers have the opportunity to learn web development skills and build a personal website. In the winter semester, these developers are paired with a team and put their skills to work on a client project.

Design

Designers get the chance to build their portfolio working with a small team and a real client. New designers also recieve attend workshops to learn instustry skills and best practices and recive regular feedback on their work from experienced student designers.
